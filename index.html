The Craft

Architecture is not merely the arrangement of components; it is the silent dialogue between logic and human intent. I believe in software that prioritizes semantic excellence over transient trends.

Every line of code is a structural choice. My approach borrows from the meticulous nature of digital curation—selecting only what is necessary, refining what remains until it achieves a state of quiet authority.
